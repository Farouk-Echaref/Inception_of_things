Berserk

*Berserk*, written and illustrated by the legendary Kentaro Miura, is a dark fantasy manga series that delves into the world of mercenaries, demons, and human struggles.

Set in a medieval world full of supernatural horrors and tragic heroes, the story follows Guts, a lone warrior with a tragic past and a relentless drive for vengeance against dark forces.

"In this world, is the destiny of mankind controlled by some transcendental entity or law? Is it like the hand of God hovering above?"

Through themes of fate, ambition, and the human spirit, *Berserk* explores the depths of human suffering and the resilience to keep fighting, no matter the odds.
