Fight Club

*Fight Club*, written by Chuck Palahniuk and later adapted into a film, dives into the dark corners of consumerism, identity, and rebellion against societal norms.

The story follows an unnamed protagonist who forms an underground fight club as a way to reclaim control in a conformist world, where chaos and self-destruction become paths to self-discovery.
